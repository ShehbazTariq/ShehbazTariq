# Shehbaz Tariq

Research Associate at the Interdisciplinary Centre for Security, Reliability and Trust (SnT), University of Luxembourg.

My work sits at the intersection of quantum information, machine learning, and next-generation communication systems, with a focus on quantum machine learning, quantum reservoir computing, quantum communications, quantum key distribution, and reliable quantum links for 6G and AIoT.

My technical stack includes Qiskit, PennyLane, Python, LaTeX, and Zotero, with experience in knowledge-graph based literature organization.
